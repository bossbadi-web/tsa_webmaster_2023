spaceship

vehicle

The spaceship is a 292-foot-long vessel1 that is designed to take you on a 6 hour flight. The spaceship is equipped with 2 360 degrees rotating stern thrusters for precise mobility & dynamic positioning. The spaceship is designed to have a low G force and give you the feel of an astronaut and view of the world.

It features a lot of glass for viewing and a shape designed for smooth landing in the ocean or other bodies of water, which can accommodate eight passengers4.

safety

Test flights are set to begin next year2. Communications throughout the operation – between land, sea, and capsule – will be provided by Silvus Technologies, utilizing military-grade StreamCaster MANET radios and MN-MIMO waveform technology3. The capsule is also pressurized3.

launch

Marine Spaceports create ideal launch conditions in two ways: by navigating to areas of good weather, which allows for year-round operations within a region, and by moving with the sea breeze, so there is virtually no wind across the deck. This enables more frequent launch opportunities3.

recovery

A splash-cone at its base will provide a smooth water landing2. A patent-pending splash-cone at its base provides a smooth and gentle water landing. Fast boats from Fluid Watercraft will then arrive to stabilize the capsule, which will be lifted onto the Marine Spaceport3.
