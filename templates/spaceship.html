spaceship

vehicle

The spaceship is a 292-foot-long vessel that is designed to take you on a 6-hour flight. It is equipped with two 360 degrees rotating stern thrusters for precise mobility and dynamic positioning. The spaceship is designed to have a low G force and give you the feel of an astronaut and a view of the world.

It features a lot of glass for viewing and a shape designed for a smooth landing in the ocean or other bodies of water, which can accommodate eight passengers.

safety

Test flights are beginning soon this year to verify that we are ready to go commercial. Communications throughout the operation – between land, sea, and capsule – will be provided by Silvus Technologies, utilizing military-grade StreamCaster MANET radios and MN-MIMO waveform technology. To provide comfort and decrease the chance of health issues on the flight, the vessel will be pressurized.

launch

We do our best to create ideal launch conditions making flights as frequent as possible. We constantly plan to move to areas of good weather and follow the sea breeze to minimize the impact of wind and minimize the likelihood of complications.

recovery

The cone shape at the bottom of the vessel will provide flyers with a smooth landing in the water. Fast boats from Fluid Watercraft will stabilize the capsule after it lands in the water so that the Marine Spaceport can lift it from the water and take it back to a launch site.
